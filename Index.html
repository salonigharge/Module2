Semester1

Enjoying a balanced life to the full. Instead of feeling at home within ourselves, the pervasive voices of advertising and social media can result in feelings of estrangement and disconnection. Research shows us that stress, depression, illness, and feelings of dissatisfaction and unhappiness are connected to a loss of balance between Body, Mind and Spirit. Learning how to slow down – in the midst of everyday busyness – is the key to turning this around.
